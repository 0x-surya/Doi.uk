How to Register for DOI ?

Any organization from any sector that is willing to make long-term commitments to the persistence and sustainability of the DOI System can apply to become a Registration Agency (RA). These organizations can be commercial, governmental, or not-for-profit. Typically, new RAs consist of a group of organizations within a particular sector collaborating to solve a common problem through persistent identification.

Often, a representative from such a community group will join as a DOI Academy member before making a detailed business case to apply to become an RA. The DOI Acadewmy Board considers applications based on a thorough review of their case. Our experience shows that successful and sustainable RAs develop additional services that add clear value on top of DOI registration for a unique new community group. Examples include:

  • An existing registry of identifiers seeking to add DOI functionality and services to their registry.
  • An existing aggregator of information aiming to use DOIs to improve services and add new features.
  • A start-up with a business model suggesting a novel DOI application, which must include significant guarantees of continuity planning due to the importance of persistence.

If you represent a community in search of a persistent identification solution, please contact the DOI Academy.
