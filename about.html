About Us

We oversee the Digital Object Identifier (DOI) system on behalf of the agencies that manage DOI registries and serve their respective communities. Identification is crucial in various industries, from tracking products in a supply chain to sharing and accessing critical information online. It is essential not only to identify an item at its creation but also to ensure its traceability over time. The Registration Agencies that oversee our operations ensure the enduring reliability of the shared DOI infrastructure.

THE DOI Academy:

ENSURING LONG-TERM ACCESSIBILITY

Our mission is to ensure that the objects our community values remain identifiable and reliably accessible, both now and for future generations. Persistent identification requires ongoing commitment to tracking objects as they move. This is where the DOI community plays a vital role. The DOI Foundation is a coalition of communities dedicated to this cause.

THE DOI Academy:

EENSURES ROBUST SCALABILITY

Built on the Handle System, created by the Corporation for National Research Initiatives (CNRI) in the early 1990s, the DOI System offers a streamlined and effective approach to registering and resolving unique identifiers. This robust system enables us to manage tremendous loads, with over 1 billion DOI resolutions occurring monthly.
